About

Subber is a startup founded in the SF Bay Area by Jasper Gan and Rohan Mirchandani. After having subscribed to a potpourri of subscriptions, they realized that managing multiple subscription accounts was a burdensome hassle. Thus, they both decided to launch a revolution in the fast-growing subscription market. Jasper had a vision for Subber, and Rohan helped to execute that vision. The vision to allow subscribers to easily access and subscribe to dozens of subscription boxes with a single account.
