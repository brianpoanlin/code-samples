Features

What's Subber?

Subber connects you with a variety of subscription services from one centralized location. Bundle as many Subber+ Boxes as you would like and receive a discount!

Then pay us a grand total each month for ALL of your subscriptions, and we will divert the money directly to each respective subscription-based partner.

Pricing

With travel agency sites such as Expedia™, you bundle hotel + flight + car for a reduced, total package price. Likewise, at Subber, when you bundle 3+ subscription-services, you save money each month.

And the more subscription-based services you subscribe to, the more money you save!

Rewards Program

A free rewards program means that you can accumulate points based on how many services you subscribe to and how long you subscribe to each of those services.

Then redeem points for free services, or depending on your tier level, you may receive freebies!

Satisfaction. Guaranteed.

It is our priority to ensure that our members find their subscription boxes on their porches on-time, and in tip-top shape.

If a concern does arouse, shoot us a message and we'll do our best to resolve the problem efficiently and to the best of our abilities.

Subscribe. Confidently.

We work directly with each of our partners meaning we transfer invoices and payments directly from your computer to them.

We also ensure that our members receive the lowest rate possible when they bundle!

How It Works

1. Register a Free Account

2. Sign-In to Subber

3. Pick and Choose your Boxes

4. Enjoy Your Boxes Monthly

5. Receive and Redeem Reward Points

So, Why Subber?

• One-stop portal to thousands of subscriptions.

• Narrow down services based on popularity, ratings, and reviews.

• Pay for all through Subber, and we’ll deal with the rest.
